Circle Serenity

// First official game focused on popping circles while being relaxing

Circle Serenity is my first official game. The premise is to pop circles as quick as possible to earn as many points as possible. Make sure to keep the number of circles to a minimum to avoid losing the game. You can find many settings to customize your experience, like the colour theme, cursor, music and much more.

Chess AI

// Chess game engine and AI project

This Chess AI is a joint partner project currently underdevelopment. The goal is to create an AI capable of ~2500 ELO on chess.com; This requires a chess game engine to be built to support our development of an AI. Additional information will come.
